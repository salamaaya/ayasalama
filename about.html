Education

I am pursuing a degree in Computer Science with an expected graduation date of May 2026. Alongside my major, I am also pursuing a minor in Cyber Security and specializing in Data Science/Machine Learning. Throughout my academic journey, I have undertaken a diverse range of courses that have laid a solid foundation for my technical expertise and problem-solving abilities. Some of the key coursework I have completed or am currently undertaking includes:

  • Algorithms: This comprehensive curriculum equipped me with a strong foundation in algorithm analysis, emphasizing the ability to calculate the asymptotic running time of standard algorithms. I grasped the significance of big Oh, Theta, and Omega notations, assessing diverse algorithms' performance characteristics, while also employing the Master Theorem to rigorously prove asymptotic assumptions. Practical implementation skills are honed through the creation of graph-based algorithms like DFS, BFS, and MST, as well as the comparative analysis of basic and advanced sorting techniques. Furthermore, I gained hands-on experience in designing and implementing advanced data structures, including B-trees, AVL trees, and 2-3 trees, and numerical algorithms such as Gauss elimination, binary exponentiation, and the simplex method. Algorithm design techniques such as greedy algorithms, dynamic programming, hashing, space/time trade-offs, reduction, backtracking, and branch-and-bound were explored in depth. Additionally, I developed proficiency in crafting comprehensive test plans for each assignment, ensuring robust algorithmic solutions, using C/C++.
  • Computer Architecture and Organization: In this comprehensive computer architecture and programming curriculum, I acquired a deep understanding of the essential architectural components like the CPU, memory, I/O, storage, and peripherals, setting the foundation for computer system comprehension. The course explored advanced topics such as parallel computing techniques for high-performance processors and delved into the broader context of computer architecture with operating systems, networking, distributed systems, and cloud computing. Additionally, I gained proficiency in Assembly language programming, mastering branching, stack operations, and subroutines while navigating development and debugging environments. Finally, I advanced my programming skills by learning C, focusing on memory management, pointers, and pointer arithmetic, collectively preparing me to comprehend, optimize, and work effectively within intricate computing environments.
  • Data Structures: This course equipped me with a deep understanding of efficient data organization, algorithms, and their implementations, allowing me to build robust and scalable software solutions.
  • Discrete Structures: By studying discrete mathematics, I gained essential knowledge in logic, set theory, graph theory, and combinatorics, which form the basis for solving complex computational problems.
  • Statistics: I have gained proficiency in statistical analysis, hypothesis testing, and probability theory, enabling me to draw meaningful insights and make data-driven decisions.
  • Introduction to Computer Science: Throughout the course, I gained proficiency in fundamental programming techniques, including recursion, loops, and conditionals. By applying these skills, I successfully developed a Connect Four game and even created a music recommendation system.
